The governance platform focused on transparent proposal creation, voting, and decision execution. I developed Rust-based smart contracts for voting and proposal management, implemented multi-stakeholder consensus mechanisms, and connected off-chain data feeds for real-time updates.

The backend work emphasized auditability, clear state transitions, and reliable service boundaries so governance activity could be tracked without hiding operational complexity from the teams responsible for running the platform.
