The DeFi protocol combined lending pools, borrowing flows, yield strategies, and liquidation logic into a cohesive backend and smart-contract system. I implemented smart contracts for lending pools with dynamic interest-rate models, integrated price-oracle driven liquidation mechanisms, and built emergency stops and access controls for safer protocol operation.

The work centered on correctness, clear operational controls, and predictable protocol behavior under changing market conditions.
